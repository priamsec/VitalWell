Cheapest Way to Get Lab Tests for Biomarkers

February 21, 2026 • 4 min read

Knowledge is power. Here's how to get your key health markers checked without spending a fortune.

Direct-to-Consumer Lab Services

Skip the doctor's visit. Order tests directly:

  • Quest Diagnostics - Walk-in, no referral needed
  • Labcorp - Similar to Quest
  • Ulta Lab Tests - Often 50-70% off retail
  • Health Testing Centers - Discounted panels

Key Panels to Get

  1. Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP) - $15-25. Kidney, liver, electrolytes, blood sugar.
  2. Lipid Panel - $10-20. Cholesterol, triglycerides.
  3. Vitamin D - $15-30. Essential for immune and bone health.
  4. Thyroid Panel - $30-50. TSH, T3, T4.
  5. HbA1c - $10-15. Average blood sugar over 3 months.

Full Longevity Panel (~$150-200)

  • CMP + Lipid + Vitamin D + Thyroid + HbA1c
  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Iron/Ferritin
  • Magnesium

Tips

  • Fast 12 hours before blood work (water is fine)
  • Go early in the morning
  • Check if your insurance covers any of these
  • Track results over time to see trends

Knowledge → Action → Better health.
