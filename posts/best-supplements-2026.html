Best Supplements for Longevity in 2026

February 21, 2026 • 8 min read

The supplement industry is full of hype. Here's what actually has science behind it for longevity and healthy aging.

1. Vitamin D3

Essential for immune function, bone health, and potentially longevity. Most people are deficient, especially in winter. Dosage: 2000-4000 IU daily (get levels tested).

2. Omega-3 Fish Oil

EPA and DHA support heart health, brain function, and reduce inflammation. Look for high-EPA formulations. Dosage: 2-3g combined EPA/DHA daily.

Affiliate pick: Nordic Naturals Ultimate Omega

3. Magnesium

Supports sleep, muscle function, and hundreds of enzymatic reactions. Many people are deficient. Dosage: 200-400mg magnesium glycinate or citrate at night.

4. NAD+ Precursors (NMN/NR)

NAD+ declines with age. Precursors like NMN and NR may support cellular energy. Evidence is promising but still emerging. Dosage: 250-500mg NMN daily.

5. Spermidine

May support autophagy (cellular cleanup). Found in wheat germ, but supplements are convenient. Dosage: 5-10mg daily.

6. CoQ10

Essential for mitochondrial energy production. Levels decline after 40. Dosage: 100-200mg ubiquinol (the reduced form) daily.

7. Vitamin K2

Works with Vitamin D for bone and heart health. Dosage: 100-200mcg MK-7 form daily.

What to Avoid

  • Most "longevity" blends with proprietary blends (you don't know dosages)
  • High-dose single nutrients unless deficient
  • Anything promising "reverse aging"

The Bottom Line

Start with the basics: Vitamin D, Omega-3, Magnesium. Then consider the more advanced options based on your budget and goals.

Always consult a healthcare provider before starting supplements, especially if you have health conditions or take medications.
