How to Choose the Right Protein Powder

  • Whey: Fast-absorbing, best post-workout
  • Casein: Slow-digesting, ideal before bed
  • Plant-based: Great for vegans, usually 20-25g protein

How Much Protein Do You Need?

Most adults need 0.8-1g of protein per pound of body weight for muscle building and maintenance. Active individuals may need 1-1.2g per pound.
